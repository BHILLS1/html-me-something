NAMASTE

namaste - a frequently used Sanskrit greeting or farewell from one yogi to another that means the abundant light that shines in me sees and acknowledges the abundant light that shines in you.

About Me

Yoga is one of the modalities that I use to show up and shine brightly in this world. I am a trauma-informed trained yoga instructor (RYT 200), Reiki Practitioner, dancer and music enthusiast. I have a passion for cultivating wellness and building connection in communities of color. I create sonically driven practices, centering her classes around musical energy. By curating and leading practices driven by music of the culture of the community that I'm a part of, I creates an environment that welcomes people of color to appreciate a yoga practice created authentically for them. I am the creator of Back Trap Yoga, St. Louis' first Trap yoga experience — a high energy, beat banging, unsuspecting approach to bring breath, movement and sonic connection together in an unforgettable way.

Back Trap Yoga

The practice of unifying mind and body through yoga, partnered with conscious rhymes and the beat banging sounds of trap music is the essence of Back Trap Yoga; an hour long, all levels, sonically driven yoga flow. Connection is the motive. Connecting to your breath, your highest self, and others makes for a transformative experience where you might least expect it. Hydrate, lay ya edges and get ready to WERK. [Back Trap Yoga is St. Louis' first Trap Yoga experience]
